# Company information
### Company name
Statful
### Product
Platform that hosts AI agents to run a company. Customer is able to pay to buy agents that run certain parts of their company.
### Customer experience example
Customer creats a company from scratch through our website (product). The customer is able to manage AI agents to run certain parts of their company. First the customer could starts with a free CEO agent, which is the general manager of the company that makes the important decisions. Next the customer could buy a product developer that takes product ideas and company directions into consideration, creats new products and improves current products.
### Statful company structure
Statful is run by AI agents that communicate together and makes decisions for their responsible department, just like the companys product.
